A Global C suite leader, mentor, coach with over 33 years of global experience in fast moving consumer goods across a diverse set of environments in both emerging and developed markets, Nandu, believes in building organizations by building people. Past roles include Executive Board Member for Nestlé in Asia, Oceania & Africa and as Global CEO for Nestlé Nutrition.

Arun has long experience in nurturing and scaling up operations of early stage companies and multinational companies in Asia. Arun was most recently the MD of Yahoo for 7 countries in Asia. Prior to that he was the MD of Monster.com for Asia Pacific & Middle East. He is an alumnus of IIT Madras and IIM Ahmedabad with over 15 years of experience in running large consumer internet businesses. Presently, he is an active investor & advisor in early stage internet companies.

Mr. Sundar has been associated with the Times Group for over 30 years across various domains such as sales, publishing, brand management and the Internet division. He was the Director (Response), CEO of TBSL and Founder member of brands such as Timesjobs.com, Magicbricks.com, techgig.com and simplymarry.com. Currently, he mentors, advises and angel-invests in start-ups. He is on the Board of Babjob.com, (A Seek.com, Australia-invested company), Advisor & Director of India Chapter of Global School Foundation (Singapore) and Advisor & Deal Mentor at Prosight Partners.

Kshitij is an experienced CEO with a track record of delivering profitable growth and market share. With over 25 years’ experience in building high performing teams across industries, Kshitij has held several senior Product & Sales leadership roles at Monster.com Asia & US. He was the founder of Mobolt, which was acquired by Indeed.com where he worked as a Managing Director for business division till Dec 2016. Currently, he is leading a profitable B2B start-up which is still in a stealth mode.

With more than 16 years of experience, Devesh expertise include leading from the front, managing large teams in a cost-efficient manner, handling all dimensions of the business and key relationship management. Currently, he is the Co-founder and CEO, Fusion Microfinance Pvt Ltd. one of the leading microfinance institutions. In the past, he has held important portfolios such as Director on the Board of all group companies of BSA Logistics Group and Head-Citigroup (credit card division).

Ashish has expertise in people management, recruitment, training, distribution and mapping productivity, spotting potential new business opportunities, structuring business plans. He has extensive knowledge in Turnaround and Change management, Strategy, Planning and Execution, process and product development. He is currently Co-Founder & Director – Altum Credo Finance and Strategic Advisor - Fusion Microfinance where he was earlier Co-founder & COO. In his previous roles, he was VP & National Sales Head – Development Credit Bank Ltd. and State Head – GE Commercial Finance

JM Financial is one of India’s prominent financial services groups, specializing in providing a spectrum of businesses to corporations, financial institutions, HNI’s and retail investors; known for its diverse businesses such as Investment Banking, Equity, Debt, Commodity Sales & Trading, Wealth & Portfolio Management Services, Asset & Alternative Asset Management, Financing and Lending and Distressed Asset Management. For more details www.jmfl.com

Aarin Capital Partners manages stage-agnostic funds with the ability to support its investee companies from incubation to exit. It seeks to create long-term value by partnering with talented, driven management teams actively complementing their skills with Aarin’s collaborative engagement thereby helping build profitable companies with sustainable value propositions. For more details www.aarincapital.com

3one4 Capital is an early-stage private investment fund with focus across multiple categories and geographies and companies with unique products & services. Through a deep involvement strategy, the fund works with founding teams to prioritize and strategize for product-market fit and to optimize growth in revenue and impact. For more details www.3one4capital.com

Singapore-based M&S Partners Pte Ltd. is a venture capital firm that invests in private equity and provides advisory services for Finance, IPO, M&A, Operations, Marketing, Business Development and global business development to newly set-up and growing-stage companies in Japan, China, Singapore, US and other Asian countries.

Brand Capital, the ad-for-equity investment arm of conglomerate, Bennett Coleman and Co (BCCL) engage with growth- oriented Indian entrepreneurs; established as well as new-gen; to provide funding for long- term advertisement solutions through unique and pioneering models, poised to provide critical leverage for brand-led growth and value creation. For more details www.brandcapital.co.in
